ABOUT ME

Hello! I am Maulana, born in Timika, May 31, 2006. I come from Papua, and since junior high and high school I studied in Malang, and then now studying at the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology.

I started to get into the world of design in high school where I joined the student council in the field of creativity, which studied canva, adobe and also coreldraw. Then when I was in college, I was introduced to several other editing software, such as Framer, Webflow, and Figma. Besides design, I also like video editing and photography, and for now I am studying 3D design to add to my experience in the field of design.
